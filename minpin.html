Old World Origins

Guten Tag! The Miniature Pinscher, or Zwergpinscher, is a distinct German breed centuries older than the Doberman. Originating as an elite ratter, they were bred for agility and a fearless temperament.

They are famous for their "hackney" gait (a high-stepping action that gives them a unique, proud elegance).
